Shri Ramswaroop Memorial University

Shri Ramswaroop Memorial University (SRMU) aims to be a globally recognized leader in education, research, and preparing ethical professionals. Founded in 1999, it offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. SRMU boasts excellent faculty, industry collaborations, and innovative pedagogy, ensuring its programs are relevant and enhance employability. With a focus on holistic development, students gain practical exposure, attend guest lectures, and participate in co-curricular activities. The university also provides scholarships and outreach programs, creating a well-rounded educational experience.

With its "Chase Reality…Dreams Will Follow""Chase Reality…Dreams Will Follow" motto, SRMU strives to set a benchmark in higher education.

Tech Fusion Club

Tech Fusion Club,TFC outlines for “Technical Combination”.It aims at enhancing the technical understanding of students other than regular curriculum. The club benefits the students by integrating their skills in creating products that revolutionize market, in the fields of Technology.

With its "Chase Reality…Dreams Will Follow""We Create, We Innovate, We Make it happen!" motto, TFC strives to set a benchmark in technical excellence.
